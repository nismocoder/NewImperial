About Me
Hello everyone,

Thank you for stopping by! Imperial Touge has been an interesting journey for me. It started with my passion for cars since I was
young. That passion evolved into being an enthusiast and owning several Japanese Imports as well as appreciating the Japanese
culture. I eventually started to learn graphic design at my job at the time and started play around with cool ideas I wanted to
create. My friends enjoyed my ideas and it motivated me to keep going! I have many ideas but not much time to create all of them!
However having a passion for cars and manifesting the creations in my mind will always be a part of what I want to do!
Thank you for following and stay tuned.
